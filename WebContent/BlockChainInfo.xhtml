About

Kindly choose your file to upload, you should upload your own unique homework file. The policy is supported by GMU Honor Code. Grading policy is posted on the course website.

File uploads are secure, you can have a look at your hashes posted in the block chain. You can also have a proof of homework submission, without sharing the actual homework until they are graded. This is a secure storage

For security purposes you need to use your private key to upload the file. The private key is given to each student at the time to registration. Please follow-up with the developers in case you lost your private key : ajain13@gmu.edu. The key ensures the security of your assignment. The block stack storage is valuable for its secure encryption policy.
